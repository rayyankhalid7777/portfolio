Cannon Fabrication

2023 | Personal Project

In this personal project, I designed and fabricated a custom cannon from raw materials using CNC lathe and milling machines. The project involved machining multiple components including the cannon head, base plate, and other mounting structures. Each part was carefully designed to achieve precision and function in the final assembly.

Component Design and Fabrication

The project began with the design of individual parts such as the cannon head, base, and supports. Using the CNC lathe, I turned the aluminum stock into the cannon head, followed by CNC milling for the remaining components. The parts were then assembled to create a fully functional cannon system.

After machining all parts, I assembled them to form the final cannon structure. The precision machining allowed all components to fit seamlessly, creating a functional and durable cannon.
